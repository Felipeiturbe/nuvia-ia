What ADMiND does

ADMiND is an AI-powered automation application designed for clinics and medical practices. It connects with your existing workflows to handle routine communication and scheduling tasks reliably and securely.

  • Patient communication via messaging channels
  • Appointment scheduling and rescheduling
  • Real-time calendar availability checks
  • Appointment confirmations and automated reminders
  • Prevention of double-booking across calendars

Built for clinic teams

Whether you run a single-location practice or a multi-provider clinic, ADMiND reduces manual coordination between staff, patients, and calendars. The platform is intended for authorized clinic personnel — no public login is required on this website.

To access the application, clinic staff use credentials provided by their organization administrator.

Google Calendar integration

When enabled by your clinic, ADMiND may connect to Google Calendar to read availability, create and update appointments, send confirmations, and deliver reminders. Google account data is used solely for scheduling-related features described in our Privacy Policy.
